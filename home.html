Welcome to the

MNP Portal


STEP 1

( If you are applying through SMS ): Send the following SMS from your existing mobile number - PORT <10 digit mobile no.> to 1902. You will receive a UPC code in reply SMS if you are eligible for porting. This code will be valid for 7 days.
( If you are applying online ): Create an account by using the "Register" option on this page and generate UPC by clicking on Generate UPC link. You will recieve a UPC code valid for 7 days on your registered mobile number if you are eligible for porting.

STEP 2

Once you receive UPC code, visit the nearest customer center of the operator you want to shift to after making any pending payment of your current mobile bill. You will receive a new SIM at the customer center and will need to fill a CAF form along with eKYC procedure. You only need to have your UPC code handy. You will be ported in a matter of minutes once you are done with CAF and eKYC.

STEP 3

Once your mobile number is ported into your preferred new mobile operator, your old SIM card will stop functioning. Now it's time to replace your old SIM by new SIM. So insert the new sim into your phone. Now you are in the new network with old number. And your chosen new Mobile operator service starts functioning


Anytime during porting you may know your porting status by logging in using your mobile number and password.
